## ‚ö° Electricity Bill Analyzer

### What It Does

The Electricity Bill Analyzer helps you:
- **Detect overcharges** on your electricity bills
- **Calculate expected amounts** based on consumption
- **Generate complaint drafts** automatically
- **Identify savings opportunities** to reduce future bills

### How to Use

**Step 1: Navigate to Electricity Analyzer**
1. Login to your account
2. Click **"Electricity Analyzer"** in the navigation

**Step 2: Enter Bill Details**

Fill in your electricity bill information:

| Field | Description | Example |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| **Bill Amount** | Total amount charged | ‚Çπ5,200 |
| **Units Consumed** | kWh consumed | 520 |
| **Billing Period** | Month and year | January 2026 |
| **Provider** | Electricity company | State Electricity Board |

**Step 3: Run Analysis**
1. Review your entered data
2. Click **"Analyze Bill"** button
3. Wait 5-10 seconds for AI analysis
4. Results will appear

**Step 4: Review Results**

**Overcharge Detection:**
- ‚úÖ **No Overcharge**: Bill is accurate
- ‚ö†Ô∏è **Overcharge Detected**: Shows overcharge amount and percentage

**Bill Comparison:**
- **Expected Amount**: What you should pay
- **Actual Amount**: What you were charged
- **Difference**: Overcharge or undercharge

**Findings:**
Each finding includes:
- **Issue**: What's wrong
- **Impact**: Financial impact
- **Evidence**: Supporting data

**Savings Opportunities:**
- **Opportunity**: What you can do
- **Potential Savings**: Monthly savings
- **Implementation**: How to do it

**Step 5: Take Action**

**If Overcharge Detected:**
1. Review the **Complaint Draft**
2. Click **"Download Complaint"** to save as text file
3. Or click **"Copy to Clipboard"**
4. Submit to your electricity provider
5. Follow up within 7 days

**For Savings Opportunities:**
1. Review each opportunity
2. Implement feasible suggestions
3. Monitor your next bill
4. Track savings achieved

### Understanding Results

**Overcharge Indicators:**
- Fixed charge discrepancies
- Meter reading errors
- Wrong tariff category
- Penalty charges
- Fuel adjustment errors

**Savings Opportunities:**
- Time-of-day tariff switching
- Power factor correction
- Energy efficiency measures
- Load management
- Renewable energy options

### Best Practices

‚úÖ **Do:**
- Keep copies of all bills
- Verify meter readings monthly
- Submit complaints promptly
- Track complaint status
- Implement savings opportunities

‚ùå **Don't:**
- Ignore small overcharges
- Delay filing complaints
- Accept bills without verification
- Miss payment deadlines
